Cucumber Salad Recipe

How To Make Cucumber Salad

Slice cucumbers and mix with dressing.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cucumbers, sliced
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add sliced cucumbers and toss to coat.
  3. Let it marinate for 30 minutes before serving.
